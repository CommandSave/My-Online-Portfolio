About Me

My introduction

My interest in web development dates back to college when a professor was showcasing wonderfully made webpages. In 2016, I graduated and worked in the lighting manufacturing and fashion industries as a graphic designer.

Years later, I made a huge pivot by going to nursing school. Throughout my clinical internships, I realized that the world of healthcare wasn’t for me but my nursing experience has added important hard and soft skills to my life that I can bring into another career. Moving forward, I decided to focus my time and energy on building technical skills towards becoming a front-end developer. The idea, despite being daunting, has never left my mind and the interest only grew more with time.

The tech industry changes rapidly, and is a sector that is challenging and innovative. My career path hasn't being linear, however the beauty of having experienced different endeavors is that I’m more confident than ever that embarking on this journey is the right one for me.
